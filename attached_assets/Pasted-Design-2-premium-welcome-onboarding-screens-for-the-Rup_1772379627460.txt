Design 2 premium welcome (onboarding) screens for the RupyaSetu mobile app.

The design must feel like a large, trusted fintech company.
It should be modern, clean, minimal, and confidence-building.
Avoid cartoonish illustrations.
Avoid clutter.
Avoid heavy text.

These screens should feel calm, premium and purposeful.

-----------------------------------
WELCOME SCREEN 1 – Speed & Ease
-----------------------------------

Purpose:
Show that recharging is fast and effortless.

Structure:

- Full-screen layout.
- Top area: minimal branding space (logo small and subtle).
- Center area: a clean, elegant illustration of a smartphone with a simple recharge confirmation or progress indication.
- Use subtle motion or dynamic feel in the illustration (suggesting speed).
- Below illustration: strong headline.
- Short one-line supportive text below headline.
- Bottom area: navigation controls (Next + small pagination indicator).
- Small “Skip” option in the top corner.

Headline idea:
"Recharge in Seconds"

Subtext idea:
"Fast, simple and hassle-free mobile & DTH recharges."

Visual direction:
Use a minimal phone graphic, simple icons (lightning bolt or progress check), and soft visual depth.
Keep everything airy and spacious.

-----------------------------------
WELCOME SCREEN 2 – Trust & Security
-----------------------------------

Purpose:
Build trust and security confidence.

Structure:

- Clean full-screen layout.
- Large centered shield icon or security symbol.
- Subtle glow or depth to make it feel secure and premium.
- Strong headline under the icon.
- Short trust-focused description below headline.
- Bottom area: a bold primary button (“Get Started”).
- Pagination indicator visible.

Headline idea:
"100% Secure Transactions"

Subtext idea:
"Your payments and data are protected with advanced security standards."

Visual direction:
Use a refined shield or lock icon in a minimal line style.
Avoid decorative elements.
Make the screen feel stable, calm and reliable.

-----------------------------------

General Design Rules:

- Use generous spacing.
- Keep typography clean and bold.
- Limit text to essential messaging only.
- No overcrowding.
- No unnecessary graphics.
- Focus on clarity and trust.
- Smooth swipe transition between screens.
- The overall feeling should be premium, stable, and trustworthy.
